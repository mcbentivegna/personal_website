About

When I was an engineering student, I found I preferred execution to engineering, and fell in love with product management. I’m most interested in helping folks in critical industries (healthcare, transportation and education) use technology to do their jobs more efficiently. I pride myself on my organization, technical skills and user advocacy.

Product Manager is a broad term. I think these are things that a product manager must do.

  • Understand and communicate user needs
  • Work with stakeholders to prioritize development work
  • Make sure development has the tools, time and information they need to build great products

As a product manager, I work best when I can also

  • Develop a deep understand of the technical background of a project.
  • Set up Agile processes and procedures to ensure projects run smoothly.
  • Contribute technically to projects in some way (data analysis, scripting, etc.)

My strengths are:

  • I run ruthlessly efficient meetings
  • I cultivate a deep understanding of technologies I work with
  • I advocate fiercely for users
